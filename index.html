About me

I am a R&T Scientist in Software Engineering at Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology (LIST). I currently work on the MOSAICO project, aiming at producing a holistic methodology and a set of solutions for the engineering and operation of communities of AIs across the SE life-cycle. This work will be integrated in the BESSER low-code platform and its associated BESSER Agentic Framework. Previously, I was PhD student in Software engineering at University of Rennes under the supervision of Benoit Combemale, Olivier Barais and Gunter Mussbacher.

Topics of interest

  • Model-Driven Engineering (MDE)
  • Software Language Engineering (SLE)
  • Domain-Specific Languages (DSLs)
  • Software Languages extra-functional properties
  • Reproductible Benchmarking
  • Self-Adaptive Systems (SAS)
